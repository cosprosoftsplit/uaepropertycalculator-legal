Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate property costs in the UAE?

Enter the property price and select the emirate. The App calculates DLD fees, agency commission, mortgage registration fees, and total acquisition cost.

Does it include DLD transfer fees?

Yes. The App includes the Dubai Land Department transfer fee (typically 4%) and equivalent fees for other emirates.

Can I calculate mortgage costs?

Yes. Enter your down payment, mortgage rate, and term to see monthly payments and total cost of financing.

Does it cover off-plan properties?

Yes. The App supports both ready and off-plan property calculations with appropriate fee structures.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All data stays on your device. Nothing is transmitted to any server.

Does this replace professional advice?

No. The App provides estimates for planning purposes. Consult a licensed real estate agent or mortgage advisor for official figures.

The App is not working properly. What should I do?

If the issue persists, please contact us at

  • Make sure you have the latest version of the App installed
  • Restart the App
  • Restart your device
  • Reinstall the App
